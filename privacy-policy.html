Privacy Policy for Kids App

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, and shared when children use our app.

Personal Information We Collect

Our app does not collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately to remove it.

How We Use Information

We use information collected from children to provide and improve our app, analyze usage trends, and personalize content for a better experience.

Sharing Information

We do not share personal information with third parties. Any information collected is used solely for app functionality and internal purposes.

Parental Consent

Parental consent is required for children under 13 to use our app. Parents have the right to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's personal information.

Contact Us
